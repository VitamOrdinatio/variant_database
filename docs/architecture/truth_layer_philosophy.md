## Purpose

The Variant Database (VDB) is founded upon a single architectural principle:

**Scientific evidence should be preserved faithfully before it is interpreted.**

The purpose of the Truth Layer is to establish the philosophical boundaries between scientific observation, evidence preservation, computational organization, and biological interpretation.

This document defines what VDB considers to be truth, what it preserves, what it derives, and what it deliberately refuses to claim.

---

# The Mission of the Truth Layer

The Truth Layer exists to preserve scientific evidence without altering its meaning.

VDB is not a biological reasoning engine.

VDB is not a hypothesis engine.

VDB is not an expert system.

Its responsibility is more fundamental.

It preserves what scientific producers observed, asserted, or derived, together with the context necessary to understand those assertions in the future.

---

# Truth in VDB

Within VDB, truth does not mean:

> "This biological conclusion is correct."

Instead, truth means:

> "This evidence statement has been preserved faithfully, together with its producer, provenance, context, and epistemic status."

Truth, therefore, is a property of preservation rather than correctness.

Whether an assertion ultimately proves biologically correct is determined by future scientific investigation, not by VDB.

---

# The Scientific Record is Immutable

Scientific understanding evolves.

Scientific observations do not.

An observation made in 2026 remains an observation made in 2026, even if its interpretation changes decades later.

Accordingly, VDB treats preserved assertions as immutable scientific records.

Interpretation may evolve.

Evidence remains.

---

# Assertions are Preserved, Not Validated

Every producer emits bounded scientific assertions.

Examples include:

* a variant observation
* a phenotype-scoped semantic prior
* a differential expression result
* a literature-supported association
* a validation outcome

VDB preserves these assertions without judging whether they are biologically correct.

Producer authority remains attached to every assertion.

VDB preserves who made the assertion, under what context, using what evidence.

It does not replace producer authority with its own.

---

# Observations and Assertions

Scientific observations occur in the world.

Assertions are producer-generated scientific encodings of those observations.

An observation may give rise to multiple valid assertions depending upon:

- producer
- methodology
- analytical context
- computational process
- evidence basis

Accordingly, VDB preserves assertions rather than observations themselves.

This distinction preserves producer authority while allowing multiple independent scientific encodings of the same underlying phenomenon to coexist without conflict.

---

# Preservation Precedes Interpretation

Scientific reasoning should always occur after evidence preservation.

Accordingly, VDB separates the scientific process into distinct stages:

```text
Observation
        ↓
Producer Assertion
        ↓
Evidence Preservation
        ↓
Evidence Persistence
        ↓
Assertion Projection
        ↓
Evidence Topology
        ↓
Convergence Geometry
        ↓
Evidence Convergence Surfaces
        ↓
Statistical Reasoning
        ↓
Biological Interpretation
```

Each stage builds upon the previous stage.

No downstream stage is permitted to rewrite an upstream stage.

---

# Truth is Append-Only

Science advances through accumulation rather than replacement.

New evidence does not invalidate preserved observations.

Instead, new evidence expands the scientific record.

Accordingly, VDB follows an append-only philosophy.

New assertions become additional evidence.

Existing assertions remain preserved exactly as they were originally emitted.

This enables future investigators to revisit historical evidence using improved scientific understanding without losing the original scientific record.

---

# The Truth Layer is Producer-Agnostic

The Truth Layer does not distinguish between evidence producers based upon discipline or methodology.

Variant observations, semantic priors, transcriptomic results, pathway analyses, literature-derived assertions, and future evidence modalities are all treated as preserved scientific assertions.

Every producer contributes observations.

No producer contributes canonical biological truth.

---

# Derived Structure is Not Truth

VDB derives useful computational structures from preserved assertions.

These include:

* assertion projections
* participant projections
* evidence topology
* convergence geometry
* evidence convergence surfaces
* query surfaces

These structures are deterministic organizational views.

They are not themselves scientific observations.

They exist to improve discoverability while remaining traceable to the preserved assertions from which they were constructed.

---

# Interpretation Belongs Downstream

Statistical reasoning systems such as RDGP operate downstream of the Truth Layer.

These systems evaluate preserved evidence using computational methods including, but not limited to:

* statistical enrichment
* Bayesian integration
* network propagation
* machine learning
* future mathematical frameworks

These systems may generate hypotheses, rankings, confidence estimates, or biological interpretations.

They do not alter preserved assertions.

Should future investigation produce new evidence, that evidence enters VDB as a new producer assertion rather than modifying historical records.

---

# Unidirectional Knowledge Flow

Knowledge flows through the repository ecosystem in one direction.

```text
Observation
        ↓
Producer Assertion
        ↓
Evidence Preservation
        ↓
Evidence Persistence
        ↓
Assertion Projection
        ↓
Evidence Topology
        ↓
Convergence Geometry
        ↓
Evidence Convergence Surfaces
        ↓
Statistical Reasoning
        ↓
Biological Interpretation
```

Each transformation enriches scientific understanding without replacing previously preserved evidence.

Knowledge accumulates.

It is not rewritten.

This unidirectional flow preserves the integrity of the scientific record while allowing future reasoning methods to evolve independently of historical observations.

---

# The Role of Time

Scientific evidence exists within time.

Scientific understanding changes over time.

The Truth Layer therefore preserves both evidence and the context in which that evidence was generated.

Future investigators may revisit preserved assertions using improved methods, larger cohorts, or new theoretical frameworks.

The preserved evidence remains unchanged.

Only scientific understanding evolves.

---

# The Discovery Engine

The Discovery Engine exists to reveal structure within preserved evidence.

Its responsibility is observational rather than inferential.

It identifies regions of evidence convergence worthy of further investigation.

It does not declare biological causality.

Discovery surfaces generate scientifically valuable questions.

Those questions become candidates for downstream statistical evaluation.

---

# Architectural Consequences

The Truth Layer establishes several architectural invariants that govern the remainder of VDB.

* Assertions are immutable.
* Preservation precedes interpretation.
* Producer authority is preserved.
* Provenance is never discarded.
* Context is never generalized.
* Derived structures remain traceable.
* Biological interpretation remains downstream.
* New evidence augments the scientific record rather than replacing it.

Every design, specification, schema, interface, and implementation within VDB must preserve these invariants.

---

# Philosophy

The Truth Layer exists because scientific knowledge is provisional while scientific evidence is cumulative.

Methods improve.

Models evolve.

Interpretations change.

The preserved scientific record must remain available to each successive generation of investigators.

VDB therefore treats preservation as a scientific responsibility rather than merely a technical one.

Its objective is not to determine what biology is true today.

Its objective is to ensure that tomorrow's scientific understanding can faithfully revisit yesterday's evidence without loss of identity, provenance, or meaning.

In this sense, the Truth Layer is not simply the foundation of VDB.

It is the foundation upon which future discovery is permitted to emerge.
